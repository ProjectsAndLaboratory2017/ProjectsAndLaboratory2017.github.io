Self-checkout cash register

Project developed for the course Projects and Laboratory on communication systems

School Year 2016/2017

Overview of the project

The slides of the presentation contain a general overview of the project. Presentation

The system is a prototype of a self-checkout cash register for a generic supermarket. The system enables users to identify themselves and scan the products they bought.
